Source Du Pays

"AFRUICA" UNITED

Planet, one of the subsidiaries of the Source du Pays who specialize in sweet beverages launched a tender to revisit their brand positioning in Africa with intentions to extend their influence into west Africa.

Planet had always been regarded as a second place to their competition in Cameroon. As such, the challenge was to create a fresh and vibrant brand identity that would speak to their target audience, in this case, the next generation of consumers - the youth.

Grammy Award-winning Burna Boy, an Afrofusion artist, loved and adored by the youth was included in the agency's brief to helm the campaign.
